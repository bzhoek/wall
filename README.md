# Paper

Generates a PDF with A4 landscape sheets that can be cut in half to get A5 portrait sheets for a planning wall.

Assumes sprints of 2 weeks.

    +---------------------------------+
    |    Sprint 1    |    Sprint 2    |
    +---------------------------------+
    |    Week 1-2    |    Week 3-4    |
    | 8 Jan - 21 Jan | 22 Jan - 4 Feb |
    +---------------------------------+
    |      2014      |      2014      |
    +---------------------------------+
